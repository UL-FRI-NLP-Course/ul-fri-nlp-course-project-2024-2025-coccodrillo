Gençlik Parkı | Ankara, Türkiye |  In the heart of the city, Gençlik Parkı has a lake and fountains (lit up after dark), colourful flower beds and plenty of space for kids to run about. It's a highly popular spot on the weekend for strolling or simply hanging out.The Ferris wheel and other rather old fun rides of Luna Park are at the southern end of the park.
