Kurtuluş Cami | Gaziantep, Türkiye |  Initially constructed as an Armenian cathedral in 1892, this impressive building features alternating black-and-white stone banding both on the facade and interior and stained-glass details.Also worth seeking out is Alaüddevle Cami near the Zincirli Bedesten. For other recently restored mosques, see the tourist office's Gaziantep History & Culture Road brochure.
