Zincirli Bedesten | Gaziantep, Türkiye |  Restored to its former glory (having been destroyed by fire in the 1950s), this vaulted-ceiling bedesten (warehouse) now houses tourist-orientated stores hawking metalware, jewellery and woodwork.Entrances are on both Kundaracılar Çarşısı Sokak and Hamdi Kutlar Caddesi.
