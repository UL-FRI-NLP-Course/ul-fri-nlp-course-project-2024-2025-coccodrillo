Karagöz Museum | Bursa, Türkiye |  The collection at Bursa's Karagöz Museum includes magnificent Turkish, Uzbek, Russian and Romanian puppets and puppet-making tools. There are also regular puppet performances, usually at 11am on Wednesdays. Check with puppeteer Şinasi Çelikkol at the Karagöz Antique Shop for the latest show times.
