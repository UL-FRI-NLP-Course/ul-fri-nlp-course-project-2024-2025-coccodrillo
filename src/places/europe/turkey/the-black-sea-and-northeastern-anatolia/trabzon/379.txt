Bedesten | Trabzon, Türkiye |  One of the most distinctive buildings of the bazaar area, the Bedestan is a 16th-century covered market hall – though, from its heavy octagonal uppers, you might imagine that it was conceived as a temple or church. Inside, the banal souvenir stores of the ground floor seem initially disappointing, but you'll thank yourself for persisting and climbing on up to the memorable top-floor cafe.
