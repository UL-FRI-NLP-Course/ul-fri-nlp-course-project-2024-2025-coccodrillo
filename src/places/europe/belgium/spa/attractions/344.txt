Parc de Sept Heures | The Ardennes, Belgium |  With a picturesque Léopold II–era pavilion and a wrought-iron marketplace hosting Sunday morning flea markets, this charming park also features minigolf, pétanque and €5 pony rides in summer.
The park’s north side slopes very steeply up through splendid beech woods, with a funicular short-cutting up to the Thermes de Spa complex.
