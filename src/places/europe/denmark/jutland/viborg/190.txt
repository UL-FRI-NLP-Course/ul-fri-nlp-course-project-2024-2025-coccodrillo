Sankt Mogens Gade | Central Jutland, Denmark |  The old part of town lies just to the north and west of the cathedral. Sankt Mogens Gade has a charming pocket of homes from the mid-16th century, including Den Hauchske Gård at No 7, Villadsens Gård at No 9A and Den Gamle Præstegård at No 11. B&Bs can also be found here.
