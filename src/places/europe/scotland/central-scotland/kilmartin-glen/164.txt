Kilmartin House Museum | Southern Highlands & Islands, Scotland |  This museum, in Kilmartin village, is a fascinating interpretive centre that provides a context for the ancient monuments you can go on to explore, alongside displays of artefacts recovered from various sites. Funding has nearly been achieved for a major redevelopment of the museum, so check the website for the latest details before visiting. It also has a cafe and a good shop with handicrafts and books on Scotland.
