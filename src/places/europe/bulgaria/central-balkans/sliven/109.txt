Obshtinski Pazar | Bulgaria, Europe |  Shop with the locals at this large warren of shops, selling everything from jars of honey (4 lv) to bags of spices (1 lv), alongside fruits and vegetables, clothes and electronics supplies. Watch your wallet, but the market is a good place to stock up on supplies if you're self-catering. The market’s entrance is located opposite ul Tsar Simeon where it meets the park’s southwestern edge.
