Troja Chateau | Prague, Czechia |  Troja Chateau is a 17th-century baroque palace built for the Šternberk family and filled with sculptures and frescoes. The permanent exhibition is devoted to the interior furnishings of the chateau; there are also rotating exhibitions sponsored by the Prague City Gallery. There’s free admission to the palace grounds, where you can wander through the beautiful French gardens, watched over by a gang of baroque stone giants on the balustrade outside the southern door.
