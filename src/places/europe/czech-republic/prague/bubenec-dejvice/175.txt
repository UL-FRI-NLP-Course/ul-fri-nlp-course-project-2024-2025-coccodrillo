Křižík’s Fountain | Prague, Czechia |  Each evening from spring to late autumn the musical Křižík’s Fountain performs its computer-controlled light-and-water dance. Performances range from classical music such as Dvořák’s New World Symphony to rousing works performed by Andrea Bocelli, Queen or the Scorpions. Check the website for what’s on. The show is best after sunset – from May to July go for later shows.
