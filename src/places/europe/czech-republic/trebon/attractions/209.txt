Regent Brewery | Bohemia, Czechia |  Třeboň's municipal brewery was founded in 1379, and still turns out excellent beers sold under the 'Bohemia Regent' label. Times for an hour-long tour of the facilities, including sampling some of the beers, are listed on the brewery website. Simply show up 10 minutes before the tour to buy tickets. Prices depend on the time of day and number of people.
