Sándor Márai Memorial Room | Košice, Slovakia |  The city's greatest literary giant is Sándor Márai, who was born in Košice (then Kassa, part of the Kingdom of Hungary). Peep at the ornate balcony of the 20th-century writer's childhood home from outside or visit the museum inside. For English-language tours, call ahead or book through Košice's City Information Centre.
