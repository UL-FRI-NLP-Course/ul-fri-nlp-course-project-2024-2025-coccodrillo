Gasteiner Museum | Hohe Tauern National Park, Austria |  Tap into the source of Bad Gastein at this museum, which spells out the town's history and the wonders of its thermal waters, from the bath-loving Romans to the Romantic painters inspired by its waterfall. The collection spans everything from Krampus (devil) costumes to vintage tourist posters and 19th-century oil paintings of Bad Gastein. English audio guides are available.
