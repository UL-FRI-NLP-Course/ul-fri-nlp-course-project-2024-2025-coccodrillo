Santa Claus Post Office | Rovaniemi, Finland |  Situated within the Santa Claus Village, this post office receives over half a million letters each year from children worldwide. You can browse a selection of the letters, which range from rather mercenary requests for thousands of euros' worth of electronic goods to heart-rending pleas for parents to recover from cancer.
