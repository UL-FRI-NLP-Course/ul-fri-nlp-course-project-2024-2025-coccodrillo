Helsinki Zoo | Helsinki, Finland |  Helsinki's leafy zoo occupies the entire island of Korkeasaari. Established in 1889 it has 150 animal species and 1000 plant species from Finland and around the world housed in large natural enclosures, as well as a tropical house, a small farm and a good cafe and terrace.
