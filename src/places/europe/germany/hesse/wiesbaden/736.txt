Marktkirche | Wiesbaden, Germany |  The Protestant neo-Gothic Marktkirche, built of bright red bricks between 1853 and 1862, was the first all-brick building to be built in the region. Its glockenspiel (carillon) has 49 bronze bells weighing between 13kg and 2200kg; it rings at 9am, noon, 3pm and 5pm daily. Schedules for regular organ concerts are posted on the website.
