Schloss & Park Oranienbaum | Central Germany, Germany |  Ongoing restoration is underway at this delightful Dutch-inspired baroque ensemble, south of Wörlitz, but visitors can access most of the rooms and in some cases, take part in the restoration process. Oranienbaum is 14km southeast of Dessau or 6km south of Wörlitz. To get here, take bus 331.
