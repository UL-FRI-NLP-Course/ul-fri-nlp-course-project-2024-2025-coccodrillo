PrinzenBräu Brewery | Liechtenstein, Europe |  This local microbrewery brews four beers – a light lager, a wheat beer, a rich 'old' beer and a creamy malt beer – as well as a schnapps known as 'Spiritus', which is distilled using a combination of the brewery's beers. Guided brewery tours include explanations and a tasting of each of the four beers.
