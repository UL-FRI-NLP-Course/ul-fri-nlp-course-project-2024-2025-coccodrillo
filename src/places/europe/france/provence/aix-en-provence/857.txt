Fondation Victor Vasarely | Aix-en-Provence, France |  This gallery, 4km west of the city, was designed by Hungarian optical-art innovator Victor Vasarely (1906–97). An architectural masterpiece, it has 16 interconnecting, hexagonal galleries purpose-built to display and reflect the patterning of the artist’s 44 acid-trip-ready, floor-to-ceiling geometric artworks. Take bus 2 to the Vasarely stop.
