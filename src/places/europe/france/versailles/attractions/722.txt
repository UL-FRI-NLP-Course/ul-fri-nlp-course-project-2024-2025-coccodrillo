Académie du Spectacle Équestre | Versailles, France |  Versailles' Grandes Écuries (Greater Stables) are the stage for the prestigious Académie du Spectacle Équestre. It presents spectacular Reprises Musicales, for which tickets sell out weeks in advance; book ahead online. In the stables' main courtyard is a manège where horses and their riders train. Show tickets and training sessions include a stable visit.
