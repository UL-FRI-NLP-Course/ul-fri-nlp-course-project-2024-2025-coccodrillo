Musée d’Art Moderne et Contemporain | Lyon & the Rhône Valley, France |  The country’s second-largest Art Moderne museum after Paris’ Centre Pompidou, this museum features an exceptional collection of 20th-century and contemporary paintings, sculptures and photographs. It's in St-Priest-en-Jarez, 2.5km north of central St-Étienne; tram 4 (direction Hôpital Nord) links it with the centre.
The museum closes between exhibitions. Check the website to see what's on.
