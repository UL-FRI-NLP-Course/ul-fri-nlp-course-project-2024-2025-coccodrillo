Pont d'Arc | Lyon & the Rhône Valley, France |  Spanning the Ardèche Gorge, this striking natural bridge is a must-see when visiting the area. Located 5km south of Vallon-Pont-d'Arc, this 54m-high arch makes a fine destination for a scenic walk (with routes beginning in Vallon-Pont-d'Arc). In the summer, it's also popular destination for kayaking.
