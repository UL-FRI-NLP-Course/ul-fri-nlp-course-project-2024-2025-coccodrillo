Grotte de la Madeleine | Lyon & the Rhône Valley, France |  The Ardèche plateau is riddled with caves. One of the most atmospheric is Grotte de la Madeleine, which features impressive and colourful stalactite formations. A light and play show adds to the thrill. Near the ticket office you'll find one of the most spectacular belvédères (panoramic viewpoints), with awesome views of the gorges.
