Chapelle de la Sorbonne | Latin Quarter, Paris |  The Sorbonne university’s distinctive domed church was built between 1635 and 1642. The remains of Cardinal Richelieu (1585–1642) lie in a tomb with an effigy of a cardinal’s hat suspended above. You may visit only on a 1½-hour guided tour (in French; €15); see the website for information on booking.
