Jardin de la Paix | Côte d'Azur, France |  Pause beneath the pines to admire the sea views from this peaceful city park. While you're at it, don't miss the obligatory snapshot of your travelling companion(s) through the empty yellow picture frame – originally part of a 2017 exhibition honoring School of Nice sculptor Sacha Sosno, it was purchased by the town and has quickly become a favourite fixture here.
