Praga Courtyard Band Monument | Warsaw, Poland |  This bronze monument by Andrzej Renes commemorates the five-piece bands (violinist, accordianist, guitarist, banjoist and drummer) who played popular songs of the day to the pubic in tenement courtyards.
On the drum 100 musical numbers are listed: text KAPELA and the chosen track number to 7141 to hear a recording of the song.
