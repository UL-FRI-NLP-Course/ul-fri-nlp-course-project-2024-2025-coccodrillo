National Museum of Przemyśl | Carpathian Mountains, Poland |  Przemyśl's impressive modern museum presents well-curated permanent exhibitions on the city's prehistoric and medieval times, its Jewish history and the story of Przemyśl Fortress in WWI (among other themes). Temporary exhibitions explore subjects such as the art of the Hutsul people of Ukraine and Poland, or the traumatic experience of the city during WWII.
