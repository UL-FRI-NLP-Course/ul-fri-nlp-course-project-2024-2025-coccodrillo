Puerto de Los Cristianos | Los Cristianos, Playa de las Américas & Costa Adeje, Spain |  Playa de Los Cristianos’ grand swathe of pale golden sand is flanked to the west by Puerto de Los Cristianos, a harbour that is home to fishing boats, private yachts and commercial boats offering everything from boat rides (including live-music jaunts) to big-game-fishing trips.
