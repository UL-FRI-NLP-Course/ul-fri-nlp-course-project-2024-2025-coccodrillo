Beckford's Tower | Bath, England |  Built as a study and library for the aristocrat William Beckford in 1827, this 120ft neoclassical tower is worth visiting for its eye-popping panoramic view over Bath. A spiral staircase leads to the top-floor Belvedere, while a small collection of paintings and artefacts explores Beckford's eccentric life.The tower is about 2 miles north of the Royal Crescent along Lansdown Rd.
