Conservatoire National de Véhicules Historiques | Luxembourg, Europe |  The construction and technology of vehicles from the 19th century to the 1970s is covered at this car museum located in a former car-manufacturing plant. Some of its two dozen or so historic cars and motorcycles on display are for sale.Admission includes entry to the one-room Musée d'Histoire de la Brasserie Diekirch, which celebrates Diekirch beer, the Grand Duchy’s best-known lager.
