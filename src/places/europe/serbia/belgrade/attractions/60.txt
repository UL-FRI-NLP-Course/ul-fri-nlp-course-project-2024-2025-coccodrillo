Museum of Automobiles | Belgrade, Serbia |  This compelling collection of cars and motorcycles is located in Belgrade's first public garage, though it was opting for new digs near Staro Sajmište at time of research (check ahead). Check out the '57 Caddy convertible: only 25,000km and one careful owner – President Tito - along with numerous classic Mercedes-Benz motors belonging to the former Yugoslavian president.
