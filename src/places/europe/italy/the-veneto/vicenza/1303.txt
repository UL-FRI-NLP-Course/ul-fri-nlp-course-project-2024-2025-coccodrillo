Palladio Museum | Vicenza, Italy |  To better understand architect Andrea Palladio and his legacy, explore the frescoed halls of this modern museum. Artefacts include historical copies of Palladio's celebrated Quattro libri dell'architettura (Four Books of Architecture; 1570) and intriguing architectural models of his lauded palazzi and villas, as well as video footage of experts discussing various aspects of the maverick's craft and genius.
