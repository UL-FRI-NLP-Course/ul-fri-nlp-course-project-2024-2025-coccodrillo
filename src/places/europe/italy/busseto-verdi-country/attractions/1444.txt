Museo Nazionale Giuseppe Verdi | Emilia-Romagna, Italy |  Take a trip through the rooms of this fine country mansion turned museum, which cleverly maps out the story of Verdi's life through paintings, music and audio guides (€1; available in English, German and French). As you explore, you’ll undoubtedly recognise numerous stanzas from classic operas such as Il Trovatore and Aida, still fresh after two centuries.
