Vietri sul Mare Beach | Vietri sul Mare, Italy |  As the Amalfi cliff-sides fall away around Vietri sul Mare, the coastline reveals its longest and sandiest beaches. Vietri's 'beach' is actually a collection of four different beaches. The best areas, La Baia and Marina di Vietri, have good parking access and are popular with families. Both are a 10-minute stroll from the town centre.
