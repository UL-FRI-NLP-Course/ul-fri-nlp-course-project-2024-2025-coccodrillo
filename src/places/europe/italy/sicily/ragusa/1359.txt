Telmo Miel Mural | Ragusa, Sicily |  Titled Preparato (Prepared), this deliciously sweet, partially abstract mural of a mother and her era-traversing child is by Dutch duo Telmo Pieper and Miel Krutzmann (aka Telmo Miel). The work was produced for the 2018 edition of FestiWall, a local street-art festival terminated in 2019.
Beside it is an abstract mural by Russian artist Alexey Luka, also produced for FestiWall in 2018.
