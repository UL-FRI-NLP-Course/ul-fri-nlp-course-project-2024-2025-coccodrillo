Lido di Fido | Grado, Italy |  A section of Grado's main beach is specially designated for canine tourists. Here they can swim, stroll along the shaded walking path that backs the beach, and rest in special doggie 'cots'. Dog bowls, dog toilets and 60-sq-m pens are all provided. Access to the beach is at entrance 8 from Viale del Sole.
