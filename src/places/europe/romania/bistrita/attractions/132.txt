Parcul Municipal | Transylvania, Romania |  With long boulevards for cycling and walking, as well as a lily pond with giant goldfish, this park is a good spot to stretch your legs, especially if you have kids. Strolling through its greenery you’ll see a statue of George Coșbuc, a poet and native of Bistrița-Năsăud county, and the buttercream-coloured Centrul Cultural building.
