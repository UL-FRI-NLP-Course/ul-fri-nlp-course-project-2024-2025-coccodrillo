Della Rocca-Barozzi Venetian Museum | Hora (Naxos), Greece |  This atmospheric museum is in a handsome old tower house of the 13th century, within the kastro ramparts (by the northwest gate). Wander through the rooms to see how the original Italian aristocrat owners lived, what they wore and how they furnished their rooms. There are changing art exhibitions in the vaults. Concerts and other events are frequently staged in the museum and its grounds.
