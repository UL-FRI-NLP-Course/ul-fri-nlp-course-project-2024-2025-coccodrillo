Lemesos Zoo | Lemesos (Limassol), Cyprus |  This small zoo is well maintained and has a selection of animals ranging from giraffes to crocodiles. Pony rides are available for kids (€3) and there's also a handy large playground right next door. The zoo is within Lemesos’ leafy Municipal Gardens, 1.5km from the central old town by walking along seafront Christodoulou Hatzipavlou.
