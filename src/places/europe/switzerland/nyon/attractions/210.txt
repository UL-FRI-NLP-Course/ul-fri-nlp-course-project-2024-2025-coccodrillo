Château de Nyon | Lake Geneva & Vaud, Switzerland |  Nyon’s castle was started in the 12th century, modified 400 years later and now houses the town’s historical museum and a rare collection of fine porcelain. Beneath the castle in its ancient cellars, you'll find Le Caveau de Nyon, where you can taste different Nyon wines by local producers. Don’t miss the sweeping view of Lake Geneva from the château terrace.
