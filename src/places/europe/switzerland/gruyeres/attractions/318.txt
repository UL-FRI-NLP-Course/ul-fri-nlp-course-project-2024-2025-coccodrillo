Musée gruérien et Bibliothèque de Bulle | Fribourg, Drei-Seen-Land and The Jura, Switzerland |  Nestled inside Bulle's romantically turreted 13th-century chateau, this museum presents a rich portrait of local history; watch for interesting temporary exhibitions. From May to September the castle-museum is a meeting point for free one- to 1½-hour guided walking tours of Bulle town.
