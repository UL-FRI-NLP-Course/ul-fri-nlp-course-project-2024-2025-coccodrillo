Valmiermuiža | Latvia, Europe |  See how one of Latvia's best-known craft beers is made on an entertaining tour: expect friendly guides and plenty of samples. Enjoy hearty pints and Latvian favourites with a seasonal twist in the Beer Kitchen, a lively cafe (open Friday evenings and from 10am Saturday and Sunday). Valmiermuiža is just north of the city centre, past Viestura laukums.
