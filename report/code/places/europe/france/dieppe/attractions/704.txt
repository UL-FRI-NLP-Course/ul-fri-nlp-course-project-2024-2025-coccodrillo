Château-Musée | Normandy, France |  Built between the 14th and 18th centuries, this imposing clifftop castle affords spectacular views of the coast. Inside, the museum explores the city’s maritime history as well as displaying a remarkable collection of carved ivory. There are also local scenes painted by artists such as Pissarro and Renoir between 1870 and 1915, when Dieppe was at the height of its popularity with the fashionable, holidaying classes.
