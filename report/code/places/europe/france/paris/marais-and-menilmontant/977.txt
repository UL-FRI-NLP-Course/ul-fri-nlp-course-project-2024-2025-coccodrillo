Musée de la Magie | Le Marais, Ménilmontant & Belleville, Paris |  The ancient arts of magic, optical illusion and sleight of hand are explored in this museum, in the 16th-century caves (cellars) of the Marquis de Sade’s former home. Admission includes a magic show and entry to the adjoining Musée des Automates – a collection of antique automaated toys.
