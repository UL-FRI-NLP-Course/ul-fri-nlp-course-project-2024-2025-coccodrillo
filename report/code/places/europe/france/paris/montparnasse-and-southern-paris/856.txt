Galerie des Gobelins | Paris, France |  Haute lisse (high relief) tapestries have been woven on specialised looms at the Manufacture des Gobelins since the 18th century along with Beauvais-style basse lisse (low relief) tapestries and Savonnerie rugs. Superb examples of carpets and tapestries woven here are showcased in its gallery. It also hosts temporary exhibitions.
