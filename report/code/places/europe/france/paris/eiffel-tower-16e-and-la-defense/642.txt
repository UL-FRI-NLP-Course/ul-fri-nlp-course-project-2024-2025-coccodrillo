Musée du Vin | Paris, France |  The Wine Museum, headquarters of the prestigious International Federation of Wine Brotherhoods, introduces visitors to the fine art of viticulture with mock-ups and tool displays in 15th-century vaulted cellars. Two-hour tasting courses (English available) take place by appointment on Saturdays (€63). If you lunch in the attached restaurant (noon to 3pm Tuesday to Saturday, three-course menu including wine from €37), museum admission is free.
