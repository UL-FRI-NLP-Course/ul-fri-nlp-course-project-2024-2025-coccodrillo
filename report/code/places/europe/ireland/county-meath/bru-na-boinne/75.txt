Brú na Bóinne Visitor Centre | County Meath, Ireland |  Built in a spiral design echoing Newgrange, this superb interpretive centre houses interactive exhibits on prehistoric Ireland and its passage tombs. It has regional tourism info, an excellent cafe, plus a book and souvenir shop. Upstairs, a glassed-in observation mezzanine looks out over Newgrange.All visits to Newgrange and/or Knowth depart from here.
