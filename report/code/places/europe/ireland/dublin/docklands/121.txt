Irish Family History Centre | Dublin, Ireland |  Discover your family history with interactive screens where you can track your surname and centuries of Irish emigration. Private consultations are also available; prices range from €60 for a 30 minute consultation to €155 for a 90 minute consultation. Consultations can be conducted in the Irish Family History Centre in the CHQ Building in Dublin 1 or online via Zoom. You can visit as part of the EPIC exhibition or buy a separate ticket.
