Na Seacht dTeampaill | Aran Islands, Ireland |  The scattered early Christian ruins known as the Na Seacht dTeampaill (Seven Churches) actually comprise just two ruined churches. The biggest is the 13m by 5m Teampall Bhreacáin (St Brecan’s Church), which dates from the 8th to 13th century. You'll also find monastic houses and fragments of several 8th-century high crosses.
