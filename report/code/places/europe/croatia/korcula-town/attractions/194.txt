Korčula Town Museum | Korčula Island, Croatia |  Occupying the 16th-century Gabriellis Palace, this museum traces the history and culture of Korčula throughout the ages. Displays cover stonemasonry, shipbuilding, archaeology, art, furniture, textiles and examples of Korčulan traditional dress. There are some interesting curios scattered over its four floors – including a tablet recording the Greek presence on the island in the 3rd century BC.
