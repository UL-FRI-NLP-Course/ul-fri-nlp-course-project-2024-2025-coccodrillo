Statue of Adolf Henrik Lindstrøm | Western Finnmark, Norway |  Adolf Henrik Lindstrøm was a Hammerfest-born cook who accompanied Roald Amundsen and Fridtjof Nansen (among others) and ended up travelling on more polar expeditions that any other person on earth, great explorers included. The town erected this statue of Lindstrøm in time for the 150th anniversary of his birth in 2016.
