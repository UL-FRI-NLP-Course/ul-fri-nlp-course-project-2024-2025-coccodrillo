Kissonerga Bay | Pafos, Cyprus |  Around 8km north of Kato Pafos, Kissonerga Bay is a long, sandy and undeveloped beach, where you can find banana plantations and solitude. There are almost no facilities, and be aware that this beach is notorious for riptides; enter the water with caution.
