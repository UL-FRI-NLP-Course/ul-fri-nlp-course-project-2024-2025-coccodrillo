Musée Archéologique de Namur | Namur, Belgium |  Housed in the photogenic Halle al'Chair, this small two-level museum houses a collection of artifacts spanning more than 2500 years. Highlights include Bronze Age jewellery, Roman-era mosaics and a Merovingian period (circa 7th century AD) skeleton with objects unearthed in Spontin (20km south of Namur).
