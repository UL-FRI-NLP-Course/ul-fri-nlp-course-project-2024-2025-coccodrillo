Bustarfell | North Iceland, Iceland |  This high-quality folk museum is set in a photogenic 18th-century turf-roofed manor house southwest of Vopnafjörður township. The on-site Cafe Croft serves home-baked cake and coffee. It's 8km off Rte 85 about 19km from Vopnafjörður (just off the sealed Rte 920).
