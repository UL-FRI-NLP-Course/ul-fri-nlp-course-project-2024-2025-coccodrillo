Laufás | North Iceland, Iceland |  North of Akureyri, on the road to Grenivík you'll find the beautifully photogenic turf houses at Laufás, a preserved manor farm dating from the 1860s. There's a small timber church from the same era. A cafe sells handicrafts, and in summer there are sometimes child-friendly horse rides offered on the grounds.
Pass (2000kr) includes entry to Akureyri Museum, Nonni's House, and Davíð Stefánsson's House.
