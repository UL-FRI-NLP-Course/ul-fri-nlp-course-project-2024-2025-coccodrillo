Suyataği Anıtı | Gallipoli Peninsula, Türkiye |  Mustafa Kemal, leader of the Ottoman 57th Regiment, spent the night of 9 August here, directing part of the counterattack to the Allied offensive. Suyataği Anıtı is past the Talat Göktepe Monument; at the top of the hill, take a right turn at the T-junction.
