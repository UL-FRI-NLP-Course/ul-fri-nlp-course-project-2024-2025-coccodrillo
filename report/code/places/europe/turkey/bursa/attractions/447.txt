Eski Aynalı Çarşı | Bursa, Türkiye |  Originally built in 1335 as the Orhanbey Hamamı (the bathhouse of the Orhan Cami Külliyesi), Eski Aynalı Çarşı is part of the historic Kapalı Çarşı and features a domed ceiling with skylights. It's a good place to shop for Karagöz shadow puppets and other traditional items.
