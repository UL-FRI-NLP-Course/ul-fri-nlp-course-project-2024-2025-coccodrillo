Kadınlar Denizi | Kuşadası, Türkiye |  Kuşadası's most famous beach is Kadınlar Denizi (literally 'Ladies Sea'), south of town and served by dolmuşes running along the coastal road. Kadınlar Denizi is slim and crowded with big hotels, but beachgoers love it for its hustle and bustle. The coast further south of Kadınlar Denizi has several more stretches of sand.
