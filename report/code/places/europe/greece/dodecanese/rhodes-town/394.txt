Roloi Clock Tower | Rhodes Town, Greece |  Commanding the highest point in the Old Town, this sturdy clocktower offers a tremendous panorama. Dating originally from the 7th century, it was damaged by an explosion in 1856, and rebuilt with baroque elements by Fethi Pasha. Visitors climb 53 steep wooden steps to reach a low-ceilinged chamber with 360-degree views. The entrance fee includes a free drink in the pleasant terrace cafe below.
