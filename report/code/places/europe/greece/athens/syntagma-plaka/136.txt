Zappeion | Syntagma & Plaka, Athens |  The southwestern third of the green space at the centre of Athens, adjacent to the National Garden, is a network of wide, tree-shaded walkways around the grand Zappeio Hall. The main entrances are on Leoforos Vasilissis Amalias and Leoforos Vasilissis Olgas.
A pleasant cafe, restaurant and the open-air Aegli Cinema are alongside the hall.
