St Luc | Valais, Switzerland |  At 1655m and a steep 5.5km up from Vissoie on the valley's eastern side, St Luc is often paired with Chandolin as their ski areas are interconnected. St Luc has its own funicular and some excellent hiking trails in summer. Views from the town include to the valley's 4000m peaks and the Matterhorn. If time permits, take the Planets Trail to Weisshorn Hotel.
