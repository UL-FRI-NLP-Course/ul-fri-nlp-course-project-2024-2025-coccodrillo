Schloss Hünegg | Bernese Oberland, Switzerland |  The plaything of a wealthy Prussian baron and modelled on the chateaux in the Loire region of France, silver-turreted Schloss Hünegg is a feast of art nouveau and neo-Renaissance styles, featuring fabulous 19th-century stuccoed salons. To reach the palace from Thun, take bus 21 to Hilterfingen (Sfr3, 20 minutes).
