Fotomuseum | Canton of Zürich, Switzerland |  The vast collection at Winterthur’s outstanding photography museum features great names and styles from the 19th century to the present, zooming in on everything from fashion to art and architecture. Additional photo shows are staged across the street in the museum's two partner institutions, the Fotostiftung and Zentrum für Fotografie. Buy a Kombi ticket (Sfr19) to visit all three.
