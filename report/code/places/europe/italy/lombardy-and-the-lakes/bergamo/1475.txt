Torre di Gombito | Bergamo, The Italian Lakes |  For a wonderful view from one of the highest points in the old town, climb the 12th-century Gombito Tower. Visits must be reserved in advance at the tourist office, which is at the base of the tower, and leave at 10am, 10.45am, 11.30am, 2.30pm, 3.15pm and 4pm on Mondays. Keep in mind it's 263 steps to the top and there's no lift.
