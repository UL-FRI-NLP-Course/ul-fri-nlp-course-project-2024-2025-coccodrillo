Museo Civico di Storia Naturale | Milan, The Italian Lakes |  This grand neo-Romanesque building houses Milan's natural history museum, complete with dinosaurs, fossils and the largest geology collection in Europe. It is home to the collection of botanist Giorgio Jan and naturalist Giuseppe de Cristoforis, which includes a kid-friendly T-Rex skeleton and a set of period dioramas populated with posing creatures from around the world. It's a fun outing for families.
