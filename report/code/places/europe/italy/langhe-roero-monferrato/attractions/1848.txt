Istituto Internazionale di Elicicoltura | Langhe, Roero & Monferrato, Italy |  Cherasco is home to the Istituto Internazionale di Elicicoltura, which provides technical advice for snail breeders (heliciculture is edible-snail breeding) and organises an annual snail festival (the Incontro Internazionale di Elicicoltura) in September.
