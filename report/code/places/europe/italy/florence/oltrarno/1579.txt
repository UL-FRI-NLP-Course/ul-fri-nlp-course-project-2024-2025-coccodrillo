Torre de' Belfredelli | Oltrarno, Florence |  This eye-catching stone medieval tower is the tallest, and probably best preserved, in Florence. It was built for the wealthy Belfredelli family in the 13th century and is today capped with the modern addition of a roof and enviable two-bedroom apartment that can be rented; search for 'Luxury Medieval Tower Apartment' on www.airbnb.com.
