Museo Civico d'Arte | Modena, Italy |  The Museo Civico d'Arte is one of two jointly administered city museums – the other beingMuseo Civico Archeologico Etnologico – inside the Palazzo dei Musei. Most interesting among its eclectic collection are the sections devoted to traditional paper-making, textiles and musical instruments.
Free audio guides are available in English, French, German and Italian.
