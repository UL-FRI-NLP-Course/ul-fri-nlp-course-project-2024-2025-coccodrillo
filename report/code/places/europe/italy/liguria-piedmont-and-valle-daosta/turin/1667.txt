Palazzina di Caccia di Stupinigi | Turin, Italy |  You can take in the hunting-themed decor at this grand Savoy lodge, some 11km southwest of Turi. It also has English-style gardens and grounds, a lovely setting for a stroll, though these may be closed.
You can get here by Sadem bus 282 from outside Porta Nuova station (28 minutes, every 30 to 60 minutes).
