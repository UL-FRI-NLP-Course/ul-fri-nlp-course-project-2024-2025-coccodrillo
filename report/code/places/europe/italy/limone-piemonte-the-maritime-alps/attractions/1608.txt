Parco Naturale Alpi Marittime | Piedmont, Italy |  Piedmont's largest park is home to spectacular landscapes, encompassing wildflower-filled grasslands, topaz lakes, shimmering glaciers and jagged mountain peaks, some of which top 3000m. There's great hiking in the area, from easy-going one-hour walks to challenging six-hour ascents to mountain huts with sweeping panoramic views.
