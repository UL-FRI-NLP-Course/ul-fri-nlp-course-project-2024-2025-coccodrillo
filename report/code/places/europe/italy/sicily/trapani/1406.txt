Palazzo Riccio di Morana | Trapani, Sicily |  One of Trapani's most admired historic buildings, this 16th- to 18th-century palazzo (mansion) enjoys a creamy neoclassical facade and many an admiring glance in the late afternoon when Via Garibaldi morphs from quiet empty street into a traffic jam of promenading locals indulging in their ritual passeggiata (evening stroll). The palazzo is not open to the public
