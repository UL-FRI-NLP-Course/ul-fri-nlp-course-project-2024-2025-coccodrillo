Lido Mazzarò | Taormina, Sicily |  This is the nearest beach to Taormina, located directly beneath the town. It's well serviced with bars and restaurants, though it gets very crowded in the summer. Private operators charge a fee for umbrellas and deck chairs (around €20 to €25 for one umbrella and two deck chairs). To reach it, take the funivia from Taormina.
