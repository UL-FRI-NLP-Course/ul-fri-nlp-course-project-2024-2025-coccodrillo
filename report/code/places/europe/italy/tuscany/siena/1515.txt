Panorama del Facciatone | Siena, Tuscany |  For an unforgettable view of Siena's unique cityscape, head up the 131-step, narrow corkscrew stairway to walk atop the unfinished facade of the Duomo Nuovo (New Cathedral). Entrance is included in the Museo dell'Opera ticket and in both the OPA SI and Acropoli passes. The entrance is via the museum's upstairs floors.
