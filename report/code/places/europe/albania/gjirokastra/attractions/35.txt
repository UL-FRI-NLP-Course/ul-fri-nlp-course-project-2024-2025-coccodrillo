Army Museum | Gjirokastra, Albania |  As well as photos of the partisan resistance against the Italians and a vast number of rifles and machine guns, it's possible to visit the cells where political prisoners were kept between 1929 and 1968. It's a humbling experience. Look for the seven windows that face the town here: 'You'll be sent to the seven windows' was a common threat in the town throughout the communist era.
