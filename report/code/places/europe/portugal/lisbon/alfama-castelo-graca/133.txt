Miradouro da Senhora do Monte | Alfama, Castelo & Graça, Lisbon |  Lisbon spreads out before you at Graça’s highest of the high, Miradouro da Senhora do Monte. Come for the relaxed vibe and the best views of the castle on the hill opposite. It’s a short walk west (along Rua da Senhora do Monte) of the tram 28E stop on Rua da Graça.
