Núcleo Islâmico | Tavira, Portugal |  Built around the remains of an Islamic-era structure, this small 21st-century museum exhibits impressive Islamic pieces discovered in various excavations around the old town. One of the most important finds on display is the Tavira vase, an elaborate ceramic work with figures and animals around the rim. Multilingual handouts are available at reception.The top floor of the museum is dedicated to temporary exhibitions with a local theme.
