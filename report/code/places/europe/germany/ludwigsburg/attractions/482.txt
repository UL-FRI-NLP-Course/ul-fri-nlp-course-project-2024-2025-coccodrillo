Schloss Favorite | Stuttgart & the Black Forest, Germany |  Sitting in parkland a five-minute walk north of the Residenzschloss is the petite baroque palace Schloss Favorite, built between 1717 and 1723 for Duke Eberhard Ludwig. It was mostly used as a hunting palace and summer residence. The interior is largely neoclassical in style and graced with Empire-style furniture.The palace was undergoing extensive restoration at the time of writing and is set to reopen in mid 2019.
