Mont Klamott | Friedrichshain, Berlin |  Officially called Grosser Bunkerberg (Large Bunker Mountain), Mont Klamott is the taller of two 'mountains' created from piled-up wartime debris in Volkspark Friedrichshain. It reaches a height of 78m, while the Small Bunker Mountain only rises to 48m. Today it is covered by trees, lawns and walking paths.
