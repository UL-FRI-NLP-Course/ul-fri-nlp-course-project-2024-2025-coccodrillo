North Yorkshire Moors Railway | North York Moors National Park, England |  This privately owned railway runs for 18 miles through beautiful countryside from Pickering to Whitby. Lovingly restored steam locos pull period carriages with wooden booths, appealing to railway buffs and day trippers alike. For visitors without wheels, it's excellent for reaching out-of-the-way spots and devising walks between stations. Book online the day before travel for a 10% discount and to sidestep ticket office queues.
