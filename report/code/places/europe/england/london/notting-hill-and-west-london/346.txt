Stamford Bridge | Notting Hill & West London, London |  Chelsea (aka the Blues) is one of London's wealthiest football clubs, and Stamford Bridge is hallowed turf for fans after a souvenir kit or a tour of the stadium. There's a variety of tours available, but all include entry to the Chelsea FC Museum. Admission to the museum alone (without the tour) is £12 for adults and £10 for children.
